Our Programmes

COHI Championship

The upcoming championship starts on 16th October, 2021.It consist of group stage, quarter final, semi-final and final. You are to register 15 players and 5 management for the team. Other guidelines, rules and regulations and included in the letter sent to you.

Register

Higher Institutions Convention of Muslim Students

An assembly of Muslim students staged for the purpose of bringing new ideas on how to improve schools’ Islamic activities. It also serve as a reportorial platform for the COHI officers which is followed by election of new officers for the next administrative year.

Campus Interactive Forum

This is a platform where students of higher institutions come together to solve general problem faced by the institutions as regards the Islamic works.

Capacity Building Course

An empowerment and enlightening event to equip students with intellectual tools required in the modern labour market.
